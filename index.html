About Me

My name is Kouassi Ngoran Abel , and I am a Mechanical Engineering student with a strong interest in aerospace engineering, robotics, mechatronics, and computational engineering. I am passionate about applying engineering principles to develop innovative technologies that solve real-world challenges. This portfolio showcases my projects, technical skills, and engineering experiences.
